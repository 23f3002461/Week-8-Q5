## Key findings
1. The company's average MRR growth for 2024 is **6.75%**, which is significantly below the industry benchmark of **15%**.
2. Large quarter-to-quarter variability is observed, with Q2 being the weakest quarter (3.15%) and Q4 the strongest (9.42%).
3. The trend shows the company is underperforming relative to the industry target in every quarter.

---

## Business implications
- With an average of **6.75%**, continued growth at this pace will leave the company behind peers and reduce competitiveness.
- Management must address both short-term revenue stabilization and medium-term growth through new market expansion and product/price adjustments.

---

## Recommendation (Actionable)
**Primary strategic recommendation:** **Expand into new market segments.**

Suggested actions:
- Identify complementary verticals or geographies with lower competition and higher willingness-to-pay.
- Pilot region-specific pricing and packaging for 2–3 target segments in the next two quarters.
- Invest in targeted marketing and sales enablement for the pilot segments and measure incremental MRR growth.
